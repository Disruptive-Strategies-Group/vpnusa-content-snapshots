About us

We know from experience that change is possible.

Violence Prevention Network USA is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization headquartered in Pittsburgh, PA. We are the US affiliate of Violence Prevention Network, the largest P/CVE nonprofit in Europe.

Our story

Violence Prevention Network was founded in Berlin in the early 2000s with a radical premise: that the most effective way to fight extremism is to work directly with extremists — not from a distance, but face to face, with respect and without judgment.

Over two decades, the organization has grown to more than 150 staff members and become the largest nonprofit in Europe dedicated to preventing and countering violent extremism. Its methods have been shaped by thousands of individual cases across the full ideological spectrum — from far-right movements to Islamist extremism.

Violence Prevention Network USA was founded in September 2022 to bring these proven approaches to the United States. RESTORE, our inaugural pilot project, launched in Pittsburgh and is now expanding nationally.

Mission

Violence Prevention Network USA fights extremism and creates a safer society by working directly with ideologically motivated individuals. We help them change their behaviors and mindsets, reject hate and violence, and embrace more democratic and pluralistic ways of thinking and acting.

Our European roots

Violence Prevention Network in Germany has pioneered prevention and deradicalization work for over two decades. Their knowledge is informed by thousands of individual cases spanning the full extremism spectrum — accompanying individuals on their journey to let go of hate and violence.

As Judy Korn puts it: "Only the continuous exchange of knowledge and expertise between the professional groups involved enables us to keep adapting our approaches to the changing needs of the evolving extremist scenes."
