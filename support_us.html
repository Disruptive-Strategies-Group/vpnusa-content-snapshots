Why give

Your support helps more people find us before harm is done.

You might assume nothing can stop extremist violence. Attacks and shootings come almost every day. But the people who commit them are few, and they rarely act without warning.

In nearly 8 of 10 cases of terrorism and targeted violence, someone close had already noticed the person moving toward violence. Usually, they didn't know where to turn.

When our coaches reach someone first, the results are clear. In external evaluations of our work in Germany, those we coached became 70% less likely to commit violence. But we can only reach them if the people around them know we exist.

Reaching someone early is far cheaper than responding to an attack, and far more humane. The FBI estimated the damage from the Boston Marathon bombing at $333 million, for a single attack. Coaching one person away from violence costs about $12,000.
