Why our approach works

We start with the person, not the ideology.

Extremist ideologies justify violence by dehumanizing others and preying on the human need to belong and to matter.

We honor those same needs by building trusting relationships that uphold dignity, refrain from humiliation, and create space for accountability and change.

We use evidence-based tools and track indicators of success until violence becomes unlikely.

Our approach is not to tell people what to think. We help people examine how they think and question whether their hate and violence-affirming ideology is getting them closer to the life they actually want.

Our story

Proven in Pittsburgh, growing nationwide

After launching a U.S. pilot in Pittsburgh in 2023, we are bringing our approach to more of the country. In the pilot we trained more than 60 frontline professionals and built a referral network of over 50 organizations. Today our helpline is national.
