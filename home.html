The challenge

Extremism is outpacing our response

Growing faster than programs

From far-right militias to online conspiracy movements, violent extremist ideas are spreading faster than available prevention programming can keep up.

Security-only approaches fall short

Threat-focused, law-enforcement-centered interventions are often ineffective at changing hearts and minds. One-on-one programs alone cannot scale to meet the need.

Communities need better tools

Teachers, counselors, and faith leaders encounter radicalization every day — but most lack evidence-based frameworks for how to respond.
