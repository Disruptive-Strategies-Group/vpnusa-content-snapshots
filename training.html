Find Your Training

Community Support Group

For people holding a hard relationship

A family member, friend, colleague, or neighbor is drifting toward ideologies that justify hostility, hate, or violence toward people who are different. You've stayed in contact, and it's getting heavy.

In monthly sessions with VPN's practitioners, you'll learn dialogue and relationship strategies for staying connected without losing yourself, and how to tell when the situation is too much to hold alone. You'll sit with others carrying similar relationships.

90 minutes · monthly · free

Trainings for Professionals

For practitioners who encounter radicalization on the job

Social work, human services, law enforcement, security, intelligence, education, corrections and reentry, violence prevention, hate crime and harassment prevention.

You'll study the current state of extremism, including regional groups and online threats, and learn what drives radicalization to violence and what protects against it. After training, graduates receive free, confidential consultations with VPN's lead practitioners whenever they encounter someone showing concerning behavior.

3-day in-person ($1,200) · six 3-hr online sessions ($750) · scholarships available

Trainings for Organizations and Teams

For organizations building prevention capacity across a team

Companies, school districts, government agencies, health systems, faith communities, nonprofits.

Our team brings the training to you and shapes it to your setting: your people, your region, the concerning behaviors your staff actually encounter. Because the team trains together, your organization gains a shared vocabulary and a clear internal path for raising concerns.

Half-day to full multi-day curriculum · on site or online · pricing based on scope

VPN Coach Certification

For experienced professionals ready to take cases

You've worked extensively with populations vulnerable to radicalization, as a social worker, therapist, counselor, clergy member, or in a similar role, and you want prevention to be a central part of your work.

You'll master VPN's methodology and diagnostic tools, and learn to guide a measurable process of disengagement. Graduates may be invited to join VPN's cadre of contracted coaches, working cases with ongoing supervision from our most experienced practitioners.

Three 4-day in-person modules over 4–6 months · $4,800 · scholarships available
