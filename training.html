RESTORE Training Program

Equip your community to prevent violence.

RESTORE connects trusted community leaders with evidence-based tools for working with individuals drawn to extremist ideologies. You don't take on a new role — you integrate new capabilities into the work you already do.

This training is for you if…

You work with people who may be immersed in or exploring ideologies that justify hate and violence

You encounter individuals becoming convinced of conspiracy theories that can lead to hateful actions

You want to strengthen people's ability to tolerate those who don't share their opinions

You can imagine working with at-risk individuals without resorting to shaming or humiliation

Who participates

Built for community leaders

RESTORE is designed for professionals who are valued, supported, and trusted within their communities. Participants stay embedded in the contexts where they already have legitimacy and authentic relationships.

Social workers School counselors Healthcare professionals Faith leaders Therapists Teachers Reentry professionals

Curriculum

What you'll gain

Understanding of different paths of radicalization to violence

Information about violent extremist groups and ideologies across the spectrum

Proven approaches, tools, and methods from trainers with decades of direct experience

How to engage with individuals who are vulnerable or already ideologically committed

A professional network of other practitioners in the prevention field

Program format

Flexible, intensive, and practical

The RESTORE training combines in-person and online components, blending synchronous and asynchronous learning over several months. The program is designed to be integrated into your existing professional schedule.
